AturaGames is a Roblox group providing experiences to players. Founded by AzuriaFrance it is originally based on European transports simulation games. Thanks to its highly experienced developer SPycre, AturaGames provide Highly optimised games with advanced content to players, constantly optimizing more and more to enhance gaming experience.
