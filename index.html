Atura Games is a Roblox group founded by AzuriaFrance. This group is focused on the creation of simulation games in the field of transport (Car, Bus, Tram). Thanks to its development team, AturaGames offers optimized and very good quality experiences for its community. Moreover, thanks to its growing community of friendly players, the group ensures the best welcome to new players, transport fans, or not. The group and its community being still very active, we ensure a long-lasting experience with many changes and events over time.

With an ever-growing fleet of over 50 vehicles, our various games offer a wide variety of vehicles to expand gameplay and ensure its longevity. In addition, the vehicles being for the majority made by blender then optimized, the group offers a very competitive graphic experience and lot of performance even for the weakest devices.
