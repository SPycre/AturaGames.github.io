Atura Transport Simulator is the flag ship game of Atura Games and also the most updated one. Basically a bus driving simulation game, this game contains a fleet of up to 40 buses to drive around the large map and Atura. Atura Transport Simulator

Atura TramCity is the second main game of Atura Games and almost as old as the group. Its gameplay is based on driving a variety of tramways trough a city. Since this game seen a very inactive development phase, expect it to be highly incomplete. Atura TramCity
